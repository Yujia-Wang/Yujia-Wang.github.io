About Me


My name is Yujia (Bobo) Wang. Currently I'm studying HCI at Georgia Tech and pursuing a master's degree. As an engineer, I'm passionate in developing interactive technologies to provide the best user experience ever.

And I'm also a designer. I love designing UI with intriguing interactions. My design is always evidence-based and user-centered because I believe the ultimate purpose of design is to better serve people who use them. To achieve this purpose, feedback from users is crucial.

I have extensive background knowledge in automation, which focuses on both hardware and software programming. I have been working on interactive technology since an undergrad. My research interests include front-end technology, wearable technology, and AR/VR. These emerging technologies are too fascinating to be used as tools, instead, the whole process should be enjoyable like appreciating a piece of art, and it is my task to make it happen.

I learn super fast. I am curious about everything. I love challenges and I always conquer.

Now I'm seeking UX Engineer Internship for Summer 2018 as a first year student. Let's rock the world, together.
